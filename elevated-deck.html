Elevated Deck

Raised structures perfect for sloped terrains or multi-level properties, creating stunning outdoor spaces with enhanced views.

Description

Our elevated decks are engineered for properties with varying elevations, creating usable outdoor space where traditional patios aren't feasible. These premium structures are built with robust framing systems that ensure stability and longevity.

Each elevated deck features our signature hardwood construction with precision manufacturing and hidden fastening systems. The raised design creates visual interest in your landscape while maximizing views and creating additional usable space beneath the deck.

Benefits

Elevated decks require professional installation with proper footings, posts, and structural components to ensure safety and stability. Our experienced team handles the entire process, from design to permitting to final construction.

These decks are ideal for sloped yards, walkout basements, or second-story access points. They can include integrated features such as stairs, railings, pergolas, or built-in seating to enhance their functionality and aesthetic appeal.

  • Creates additional usable space underneath the deck
