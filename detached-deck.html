Detached Deck

Stand-alone structures that create a perfect relaxation area in your yard, designed to be a destination within your outdoor space.

Description

Our detached decks are specially designed as freestanding structures that can be placed anywhere in your yard or garden. These versatile platforms create dedicated spaces for relaxation, entertainment, or outdoor dining without being attached to your home.

Each detached deck is crafted from premium hardwood with meticulous attention to detail. The modular design allows for custom shapes and sizes to fit your specific space and usage needs, from intimate garden retreats to spacious entertainment platforms.

Benefits

Detached decks offer unparalleled flexibility in placement and design. They can be installed on various surfaces, including grass, gravel, or concrete, and can be positioned to capture the best views, sunlight, or shade in your outdoor space.

The elevated design provides better drainage and air circulation beneath the deck, extending its lifespan while creating a distinct visual element in your landscape. The premium hardwood construction ensures durability against weather and daily use.

Design & Customization

  • Create a destination within your landscape
  • Position anywhere in your yard to maximize views or create privacy
  • Can include built-in features like benches, planters, or pergolas
  • Perfect for creating garden retreats or meditation spaces
  • Minimal ground preparation required compared to attached decks
