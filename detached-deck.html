Detached Deck

Stand-alone structures that create a perfect relaxation area in your yard, designed to be a destination within your outdoor space.

Description

A detached deck (also called a platform deck or floating deck) is a freestanding platform dettached from a house.

It is supported (or "floats") on a foundation of concrete blocks or a bed of compacted gravel, allowing for flexible placement around your property.

Benefits

Create New Outdoor Spaces

Since they are not attached to your home and can be positioned anywhere within your property, these effectively create new and completely separate living spaces.

Flexibility

Floating decks expand your outdoor design possibilities, and comply with different location configurations and restrictions.

Architectural Impact and Property Value

Well-designed decks can significantly boost the appeal, overall aesthetic appeal, and market value of your property.

Design & Customization

To design your detached deck, we only need:

  1. The desired shape (Rectangular, L-Shaped, etc.)
  2. Shape measurements in feet
  3. The number of levels (single level, multi level, etc.)
  4. Height above ground level (if any)
  5. Additional elements like stairs and railings (if any)
  6. Terrain characteristics

You can provide us with this information, or our team can visit the installation site to evaluate the project.
