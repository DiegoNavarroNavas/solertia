Rooftop & Balcony Deck

Transform your rooftop or balcony into a beautiful outdoor living space with our premium modular decking solutions.

Description

Our rooftop and balcony decks are specially engineered for elevated urban spaces, transforming unused areas into stunning outdoor retreats. We use lightweight yet durable materials that won't overburden the structure beneath.

Each deck features our signature precision manufacturing with modular components that can be easily transported to upper levels and assembled on-site. The premium hardwood construction provides a luxurious feel while withstanding harsh rooftop conditions including sun exposure, wind, and temperature fluctuations.

Benefits

Rooftop and balcony installations require specialized knowledge of load requirements, drainage considerations, and appropriate fastening systems. Our expert team conducts thorough structural assessments and works with building management when necessary to ensure safe, code-compliant installations.

Our innovative pedestal system creates a level surface over uneven roofing or balcony floors while providing proper drainage underneath. This system also allows for easy access to the waterproofing membrane for maintenance or repairs without dismantling the entire deck.

Design & Customization

  • Engineered to meet weight restrictions of rooftop and balcony applications
  • Modular design allows for custom configurations in challenging spaces
  • Provides enhanced comfort compared to concrete or tile surfaces
  • Weather-resistant materials suitable for exposed conditions
