Structural Excellence

Our decking system has undergone rigorous structural analysis to ensure it can withstand various load conditions. Our analysis considers critical loads, such as dead and live loads, and assesses the design safety factors and deformations.

Design Characteristics

The deck floor is designed as a slab supported by beams. The joists are spaced 1 ft apart, providing multiple supports for the deck floor.

Calculation Methods

The calculations consider factors such as flexural stresses, compressive stresses, and deformations.
