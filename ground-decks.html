Ground Deck

The perfect solution for flat areas and gardens with minimal elevation, offering seamless indoor-outdoor transitions with premium quality and design.

Description

Our ground decks are crafted from premium hardwood, engineered for durability and designed to complement your outdoor space. The low-profile design sits directly on a prepared base, providing a seamless transition from indoor to outdoor spaces.

Each deck features our signature precision manufacturing with perfectly matching components and hidden fastening systems, resulting in a clean, professional appearance that enhances your garden or patio area.

Benefits

Ground decks are quick to install on a variety of surfaces including concrete, compacted gravel, or a properly prepared soil base. They're ideal for areas where minimal height is desired, such as patio extensions, garden walkways, or around pool areas.

Our expert team ensures proper drainage and ventilation to extend the life of your deck, while the premium hardwood construction resists rot, insects, and weather damage, requiring minimal maintenance over its long lifespan.

Design & Customization

  • No permit requirements in most areas (always check local codes)
  • Quick and straightforward installation
  • Accessible design with no steps or elevation changes
  • Perfect for smaller spaces or adding usable outdoor area
  • Modular design allows for future expansion or reconfiguration
