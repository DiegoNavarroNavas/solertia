Ground Deck

The perfect solution for flat areas and gardens with minimal elevation, offering seamless indoor-outdoor transitions with premium quality and design.

Description

A ground deck is installed close to the earth's surface.

It is designed to sit just above ground levelâ€”often without the need for extensive footings or railings.

Ground decks provide a seamless transition between indoor and outdoor spaces.

Benefits

Seamless Outdoor Integration

Their design helps blend the deck into the existing landscape, ideal for patios or casual outdoor dining.

Versatility

Perfect for small gardens, patios, or as an additional low-profile entertaining area.

Easier to Build

Their low profile means fewer materials, simpler foundations, and, in many cases, no permit requirement.

Architectural Impact and Property Value

Well-designed decks can significantly boost the appeal, overall aesthetic appeal, and market value of your property.

Design & Customization

To design your ground deck, we only need:

  1. The desired shape (Rectangular, L-Shaped, etc.)
  2. Shape measurements in feet
  3. Railing requirements (if any)
  4. Terrain characteristics

You can provide us with this information, or our team can visit the installation site to evaluate the project.
